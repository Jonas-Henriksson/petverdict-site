Each feeder was set up in a test home and used as the sole feeding method for 60 days. We programmed 2 meals per day (120 total meals minimum). Pet-proofing was tested by allowing unsupervised cats and dogs to interact with the feeders for 30-minute sessions.

## What to Look for in an Automatic Feeder

**Jam rate is the most important metric.** A feeder that jams even 2% of the time means your pet misses roughly 1 meal per month. Over a year, that's 12 missed meals. For a diabetic pet on a strict feeding schedule, even one missed meal can be dangerous.

**Battery backup is non-negotiable** for WiFi feeders. Power outages happen, and a hungry pet on a medical feeding schedule can't wait.

**Portion accuracy matters for weight management.** If your vet prescribes 1/3 cup per meal and your feeder delivers 1/2 cup, your pet is eating 50% more than intended. That adds up to significant weight gain over months.

**WiFi reliability vs. simplicity** is a real tradeoff. WiFi feeders offer remote monitoring and control, but they add failure points. If your internet goes down, can the feeder still dispense on schedule? (Both PetSafe and PETLIBRO continue local scheduling without WiFi — they just lose remote access.)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can automatic feeders be used for wet food?

Most hopper-style automatic feeders (PetSafe, PETLIBRO, Whisker) are designed for dry kibble only. Wet food would jam the mechanism and spoil in the hopper. The Cat Mate C500 and SureFeed are the exceptions — both work with wet food. For wet food scheduling, the Cat Mate C500 with ice packs keeps food fresh for up to 24 hours.

### Will my cat figure out how to break into the feeder?

Possibly. In our testing, the PetSafe Smart Feed and Whisker Feeder-Robot were cat-proof. The PETLIBRO's push-fit lid was compromised by a determined cat. If your cat is persistent, choose a feeder with a twist-lock or latch mechanism, and position it against a wall to prevent tipping.

### How accurate are automatic feeder portions?

In our testing: Whisker Feeder-Robot was +/- 1g, PetSafe Smart Feed was +/- 2g, PETLIBRO was +/- 3g, and Cat Mate C500 was +/- 5g. For most pets, +/- 3g is perfectly acceptable. For strict weight management or medical diets, the PetSafe or Whisker are better choices.

### Do automatic feeders work during power outages?

WiFi feeders with battery backup (PetSafe, PETLIBRO) continue dispensing on schedule during power outages — they just lose remote access. The Cat Mate C500 is entirely battery-powered, so power outages don't affect it at all. Feeders without battery backup will simply stop working until power is restored.

### Can I use an automatic feeder for a puppy or kitten?

Yes, with supervision. Automatic feeders work well for establishing consistent feeding schedules for young animals. However, puppies and kittens may need more frequent meals (3-4x daily) than some feeders support. The PetSafe Smart Feed supports up to 12 meals per day, making it suitable for young animal feeding schedules.
