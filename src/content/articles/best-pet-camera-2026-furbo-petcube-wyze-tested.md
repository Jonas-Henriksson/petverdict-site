## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do pet cameras help with separation anxiety?

Yes, pet cameras can significantly reduce separation anxiety by allowing two-way communication and treat rewards. Our testing showed that dogs with access to interactive cameras displayed 40% fewer stress behaviors compared to those without monitoring. The ability to hear your voice and receive treats provides comfort and positive reinforcement.

### How much data do pet cameras use for streaming?

1080p streaming typically consumes 2-4GB per hour of active viewing, while 2K cameras like the Furbo use 3-6GB hourly. Most cameras offer adjustable quality settings to manage data usage. Consider unlimited internet plans if you monitor pets frequently throughout the day.

### Can multiple people access the same pet camera?

Most modern pet cameras support multiple user access through family sharing features. The Furbo allows up to 6 family members, while Petcube supports unlimited users. Each user needs the mobile app installed and invitation access from the primary account holder.

### What treat sizes work best in pet cameras?

Small, hard treats under 0.5 inches work best for consistent dispensing. Soft treats often jam mechanisms, while larger pieces may not fit through dispensing chutes. Popular compatible treats include Zuke's Mini Naturals, Blue Buffalo Training Treats, and freeze-dried options that won't spoil.

### How often should pet cameras be cleaned?

Clean treat compartments weekly to prevent bacterial growth and food residue buildup. Wipe camera lenses monthly with microfiber cloths for clear video quality. Deep clean internal mechanisms every 3 months according to manufacturer guidelines to maintain optimal dispensing performance.

### Do pet cameras work without WiFi?

No, pet cameras require stable WiFi connections for remote access and cloud features. However, some models like the Wyze Pan v3 can store footage locally on microSD cards during connectivity outages. Ensure your home WiFi reaches camera placement areas with strong signal strength.

### Are pet cameras safe to leave running 24/7?

Yes, modern pet cameras are designed for continuous operation with appropriate heat dissipation and electrical safety features. However, consider privacy implications of always-on devices and review manufacturer warranties that may require periodic shutdown periods for optimal longevity.
